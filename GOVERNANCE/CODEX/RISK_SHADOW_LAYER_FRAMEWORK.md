# RISK / SHADOW LAYER FRAMEWORK

## Purpose
Defines the risk layer as the counterbalance that prevents cascade failure.

## Visible Artifacts
- Red-team logs
- Risk registers

## Hidden/Latent Concerns
- Edge-case vulnerabilities
- Latent bias
- Adversarial exploitation

## Function
Risk/shadow systems provide containment, detection, and escalation pathways when governance constraints are threatened.
